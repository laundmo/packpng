The Panorama Side-Project
to find the seed of the Minecraft main-menu background image from minecraft version beta 1.8 through version 1.12

This side-project was started by Tomlacko on SalC1's Discord Channel. While the search for pack.png was still up and running, Tomlacko went out to search the second most memorable picture in minecraft, which was in the game for eight years! The panning background image in Minecraft's main-menu. Together with the help of other seed reverse-engineers and the help of crowd-sourced computational power through Minecraft@Home the seed was found.


These are the seeds:

2151901553968352745 or 8091867987493326313 (Yes, there are two and they both work!)
Coordinates: X=61, Y=75, Z=-68
Version: Beta 1.7.3 (or earlier)
